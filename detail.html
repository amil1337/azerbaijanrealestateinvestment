Property Description

This exceptional luxury villa located in the prestigious Badamdar district of Baku offers a perfect blend of modern elegance and comfort. Set on an elevated position with breathtaking views of the Caspian Sea, this is an extraordinary opportunity for the discerning investor looking for premium rental yield or a high-end family residence.

Meticulously designed with attention to detail, the property features expansive living spaces flooded with natural light through floor-to-ceiling windows. The open-concept main floor includes a grand reception area, formal dining room, and a state-of-the-art gourmet kitchen equipped with high-end appliances and custom cabinetry.

The outdoor space is equally impressive with a private swimming pool, landscaped garden, and multiple terraces perfect for entertaining or relaxing while enjoying the panoramic views. The property also includes a private garage, security system, and smart home technology throughout.

Available for long-term rental, this prestigious property represents the pinnacle of luxury living in one of Baku's most sought-after neighborhoods, offering excellent proximity to international schools, diplomatic residences, and premium shopping destinations.
